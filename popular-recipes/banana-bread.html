Banana Bread

Nutrition Facts

Per Serving:

Protein 3.8g
Carbohydrates 34.8g
Fat 8.8g
Cholesterol 51.3mg
Sodium 222.9mg
Calories 229

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup butter
  • ¾ cup brown sugar
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 2⅓ cups mashed overripe bananas

Directions

  1. Step 1

    2. Preheat oven to 350 F° (175 C°). Lightly grease a 9x5 inch loaf pan.

  2. Step 2

    3. In a large bowl, combine flour, baking soda and salt. In a separate bowl, cream together butter and brown sugar. Stir in eggs and mashed bananas until well blended. Stir banana mixture into flour mixture; stir just to moisten. Pour batter into prepared loaf pan.

  3. Step 3

    4. Bake in preheated oven for 60 to 65 minutes, until a toothpick inserted into center of the loaf comes out clean. Let bread cool in pan for 10 minutes, then turn out onto a wire rack.
